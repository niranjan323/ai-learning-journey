# 03 — Generative AI Fundamentals

A conceptual foundation: what generative AI is, what it can and can't do,
and the key terms you'll see everywhere.

> 📦 **Course:** [Generative AI for Everyone](https://learn.deeplearning.ai/courses/generative-ai-for-everyone) · ~10 hrs · Jun 17 – Jun 20

## 🎯 Learning Objectives

- Define generative AI and distinguish it from traditional ML
- Understand common modalities (text, image, audio, code)
- Know core terminology: model, parameters, tokens, context window
- Understand strengths, limitations, and risks (hallucination, bias)
- Recognize real-world use cases

## 🧩 Key Concepts

- **Generative vs. discriminative** models
- **Foundation models** and emergent capabilities
- **Context window** and why it matters
- **Hallucination** and how to mitigate it
- **Responsible AI** — bias, safety, and ethics

## 💡 Use Cases

- Content generation & summarization
- Question answering & search
- Coding assistance
- Translation & rewriting
- Conversational assistants
